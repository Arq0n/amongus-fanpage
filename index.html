This is an Among Us Fanpage

Among Us is an online multiplayer social deduction game developed and published by American game studio Innersloth. The game was inspired by the party game Mafia and the science fiction horror film The Thing.

Characters

Red

Red is generally considered the "sus" character in Among Us. They are systematically "sus" and should be kept track of.

Green

While Red is considered the "sus" of the bunch, Green is usually the imposter. Beware of Green when you encounter them.

Pink

Pink keeps everyone in line by keeping track of them constantly. Haven't completed all your tasks? Pink knows. Big brother is always watching.

Black

Won't ever shut up. Constantly talking even though nobody can hear them and their info is generally useless. Always vote them off first.
